Agenda

Here you can create an agenda for your event.

Show the attendees what is the schedule. During creation you can choose kinds of sessions, their start and end time.

If the event happens in more than one room, it is good to fill in the Track field to let people know where they can find sessions they are interested in.

You can choose sessions from the existing list of presentations on your event or name them on your own.
